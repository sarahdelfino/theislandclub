Event Rules

  • Unlawful activity is prohibited.
  • Events must end by 11pm.
  • Sparklers are not allowed at the Sullivan's Island Club due to safety issues.
  • No artificial flower petals, confetti or feathers are allowed. These items are hazardous to the environment around the Club.
  • Personal fire pits are strictly prohibited.
  • No smoking or pets are allowed in the venue, with the exception of service animals.
  • If loud music is being played the doors and windows must remain shut.
  • Events must be held inside the building unless special permission is given by the club management, if it is permitted the renter will provide their own liability insurance and permits from the town of Sullivans Island.
  • All items brought to the venue must be removed at the conclusion of your event before you leave the Club. Cleanup or removal of items left overnight may impact the security deposit refund.
  • The limit of guests allowed at an event is 60 people, any larger gathering must have club management approval and may require the renter to provide their own insurance and a larger security deposit.
  • The club reserves the right to end any event if any of the above rules are broken and the security deposit will be forfeited.
