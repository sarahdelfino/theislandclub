Capital Project

Our goal is simple:
We want to provide a place to bring neighbors together.

To be a successful community center, the building needs to be safe, attractive, and functional.

Our fundraising goal of $250,000 would transform the club into something our community can be proud of.

19% of our goal has been raised so far. Please donate today!

We've worked with local architect Steve Herlong and David Lugo of Paverscapes to come up with a plan to achieve these goals

  • Restore the center door and add a porch
  • Improve the patio under the oak tree
  • Add a new ADA ramp and ada parking space
  • Add a front yard by replacing the concrete with pervious pavers and grass
  • Complete interior update to the restrooms, bar, fixtures, and furnishes
  • Add hot water
  • Add insulation
